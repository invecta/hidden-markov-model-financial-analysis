Problem Statement

Market Volatility Challenge

Financial markets operate in hidden "regimes" that traditional analysis methods fail to capture:

  • Bull Market: Rising prices, high confidence
  • Bear Market: Falling prices, low confidence
  • Sideways Market: Range-bound trading, uncertainty

Traditional Approach Limitations

  • Linear models assume constant relationships
  • Technical indicators provide lagging signals
  • Fundamental analysis doesn't capture regime changes
  • Most models ignore hidden state structure

HMM Solution Benefits

  • State Identification: Automatically identify hidden market states
  • Transition Modeling: Capture how markets move between states
  • Probabilistic Predictions: Provide confidence measures
  • Adaptive Learning: Update models as new data arrives
